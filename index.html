About Me

My creative journey began in Russia, where I was born in 1983. In 2013, a new chapter brought me to the beautiful landscapes of the Netherlands—a move that would deeply influence my artistic vision. Driven by a desire to master the timeless techniques of the greats, I pursued my studies at the prestigious Groningen Klassieke Academie, where I proudly earned my Master’s degree in 2024.

Today, I am honored to be a member of the Royal Institute of Oil Painters. While oil portraiture is my deepest passion—capturing the raw emotion and soul of a subject on canvas—my artistic expression doesn't stop at the easel. I am equally fascinated by the tactile world of three-dimensional art, frequently working in clay and wax modeling to bring form to life.

For me, art is not just a personal pursuit; it is a gift to be shared. Every Sunday, I open the doors of my studio to teach the next generation of young artists, helping children discover the joy of creating. I also take great pride in organizing masterclasses in my studio, bringing master artists from all around the world to share their techniques with our local community.

Whether through the stroke of a brush, the molding of clay, or guiding a student, my goal is always the same: to find and share the beauty in the world around us. Thank you for being here, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work.
